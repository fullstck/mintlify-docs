<Update label="July 2025">
  **Enhanced Location Features**

  - Added `geoAccuracy` field for GPS coordinate precision indicator
  - New `withLocation` filter to find properties with GPS coordinates
  - Polygon search for custom geographic areas and neighborhood targeting with `geoShapes` field  

  **Enhanced Prices Filtering**

  - Added `advertPriceMin` and `advertPriceMax` to filter properties based on any non expired adverts prices.
</Update>
